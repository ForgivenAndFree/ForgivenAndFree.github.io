# Resources

Additional reading to deepen your understanding of the theological foundations behind this book.

---

## Articles

- [**Why Lutheranism: Assurance, Despair, and the Architecture of Salvation**]({{ site.baseurl }}/resources/why-lutheranism)
  Why the theological tradition behind this book matters — how Reformed covenant theology generates the very despair and uncertainty that keeps people enslaved, and how Lutheran theology provides the external, objective ground on which freedom actually stands.

- [**A Guide to Private Confession and Absolution**]({{ site.baseurl }}/resources/private-confession)
  Most Lutherans don't know this gift exists. What Luther taught about private confession, why it matters for pornography specifically, what to expect when you go, and how to make it a regular practice.

- [**Scripture and Catechism for the Moment of Temptation**]({{ site.baseurl }}/resources/scripture-and-catechism)
  Gospel passages and Catechism quotes organized by the specific lie you're facing — identity attacks, the big monster's promises, feelings of weakness, shame, and the "missing out" lie. Includes a rapid-response section for active temptation.
